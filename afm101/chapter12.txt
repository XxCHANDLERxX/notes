Chapter 12
Reporting and Interpreting Shareholders' Equity

Authorized Shares
    Maximum number of shares the company can sell to the public.

Issued Shares
    Shares that have been issued.

Outstanding Shares
    Shares not issued yet.

Common Shares
    Shares for the common people.

Preferred Shares
    Shares that get the first cut.
    Do not grant voting rights.
    Unpaid dividends in arrears accumulate.

Dividends
    Declaration date
    Date of record
    Payment date
